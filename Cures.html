Cures For Loneliness

1.Be comfortable with your thoughts and feelings -When alone you can help yourself by being calm and thoughtful of your loneliness or try to make things better.

2.Loneliness can also be attributed to internal factor such as low self-esteem. People who lackk confidence in themselves often believe that they are unworthy of the attention or regard of other people, which can lead to isolation and chronic loneliness.

3.Confort your mind set - When alone you can keep your mind with thoughts to keep you focus and calm and you can fulfil and make yourself whole.

4.Everyone is Connected -When in situations you can meet new people and connect with them to help you overcome and forget loneliness.

5.Do what you like -Do thing you like and enjoy it go places, have fun, meet new people

6.Don’t play the comparison game -Don’t compare yourself with famous rich people just be yourself and have fun.
