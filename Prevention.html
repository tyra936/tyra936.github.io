Prevention of Loneliness

Loneliness can be overcome. It does require a conscious effort on your part to make a change. In the long run, making a change can make you happier, healthier and enable you impact others around you in a positive way.

Consider Community service or another activity that you enjoy. These situation present great opportunities to meet people and cultivate new friendships and social interaction. Lonely people often expect rejection, so instead focus on the positive thoughts and attitude in your social relationship.

Focus on developing quality relationships. Seek people who share similar attitudes, interest, and values with you.

Recognize that loneliness is a sign that something needs to change. Understand the effects of loneliness in your life as there are physical and mental repercussion for loneliness.
