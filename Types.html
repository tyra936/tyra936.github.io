Types of Lonliness

Social Loneliness - Is the objective physical separation from other people (living alone) while loneliness is the subjective distressed feeling of being alone separated. Its possible to feel lonely while among other people, and you can be alone yet not feel lonely.

Emotional Loneliness-It arises from the feeling that you lack relationship or attachment. You might experience emotional loneliness when everyone but you has a romantic partner in your group.

Existential Loneliness-Is form an existential perspective a little bit of existential loneliness is good for your soul, and it is definitely an inevitable part of the human experience .However ,loneliness tends to stir up negative feelings, and while those can be helpful in the terms of self-exploration. They’re also something to which we are adverse and want to avoid as much as we can.

Post-Breakup Loneliness-When you go from seeing the same person every day, to no longer having them in your life at all, it can create a strong feeling of loneliness that’s difficult to ignore. In fact "the process of breaking up can sometimes be compared to the death of a loved one,"

Caregiver Loneliness-There're a very specific loneliness that can creep in when you're a responsible for the care of another person be it an elderly parent, a sick sibling or a disabled partner. Not only is there a lot of stress associated with caregiving ,but it can also be isolating.
